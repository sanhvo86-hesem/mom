## 11. Quy tac ap dung

1. **Tat ca viet tat trong danh sach nay PHAI giu nguyen tieng Anh** khi xuat hien trong moi tai lieu QMS.
2. **Lan dau xuat hien** trong tai lieu: ghi day du "Ten day du (Viet tat)". Vi du: "First Article Inspection (FAI)".
3. **Cac lan sau**: chi dung viet tat. Vi du: "FAI".
4. **Trong bang va bieu mau**: luon dung viet tat de tiet kiem khong gian.
5. **Ten san pham** (Epicor, SharePoint, Power BI...): luon giu nguyen, KHONG viet thuong, KHONG viet tat them.
6. **Ten vat lieu** (PEEK, Vespel, Hastelloy...): luon giu nguyen ten thuong mai.
7. **Ten phuong phap** (Dreyfus, Pareto, Kolb...): luon giu nguyen ten goc.
8. Khi viet tai lieu moi, kiem tra danh sach nay truoc de dam bao nhat quan.
9. Neu can them viet tat moi vao danh sach: gui DCR (FRM-102) de QMS Engineer cap nhat.
10. Danh sach nay la **tai lieu song** — cap nhat khi co thuat ngu moi duoc su dung trong he thong.
