HESEM MOM CNC job-order dashboard study 27/03/2026

Research to blueprint

Blueprint giao diện cho 2 dashboard CNC job-order

Study nay chuyen hai man hinh hien tai tu document portal thanh control surface cho xuong CNC. Muc tieu la nhin thay cung luc don hang, may, quality, revision, traceability va owner cua moi blocker.

Inference tu Siemens, SAP, Tulip, MachineMetrics, ProShop Dashboard chuan van hanh khong bat dau bang thu vien tai lieu; no bat dau bang exception va quyet dinh.
Visual thesis

Industrial control wall, it mau, it chrome, nhan manh risk. Flowboard va heatmap la visual anchor thay vi stat card.

Content plan

Dashboard 1 uu tien live order, may, hold point. Dashboard 2 uu tien phase, readiness, blocker, owner va evidence.

Interaction thesis

Click job mo inspector. Click phase doi focus gate. Role filter doi trong tam hien thi ma khong lam roi layout.

Mobile deployment control

Mobile khong can heatmap day du. Can blocked department list, owner, due time va next gate.

Rule 1

Mot viewport chi tra loi mot cau hoi. Live mobile tra loi job nao can can thiep. Deploy mobile tra loi gate nao chua qua.

Rule 2

Khong dem ca heatmap va KPI grid xuong dien thoai. Gom thanh list co owner, due time va status.

Rule 3

Moi selected object chi giu 3 action. Neu hon 3, nguoi dung se quay lai desktop de thao tac.
