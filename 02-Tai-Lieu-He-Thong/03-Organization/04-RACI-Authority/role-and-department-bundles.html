ORG-BUNDLE-001 - Role and Department Bundles

Tai lieu nay khoa nghia cua cac bundle trong mo hinh job-order CNC. Moi bundle phai truy ve duoc JD / D-code goc, co pham vi dung ro, va khong duoc dung de che mo authority hay named accountability.

Rule bat buoc:
Neu tai lieu da xac dinh duoc actor cu the thi phai dung role code hoac D-code. Chi dung bundle khi trach nhiem that su la lop actor lap lai da duoc cong bo. Moi bundle token dung doc lap trong noi dung hien thi phai link ve trang nay.

2. Nguyen tac doc bundle

  • Bundle khong tao ra JD moi. Moi chip trong bundle van phai truy ve duoc JD hoac handbook goc.
  • Bundle cap role dung cho layer actor; bundle cap department dung cho mandate cap phong ban; bundle mixed chi dung khi tai lieu dang noi toi lop enablement hoac interface lien phong ban that su on dinh.
  • Neu mot o owner / approver / hold-release cell da biet ro actor cu the, phai bo bundle va doi ve role code / D-code dung layer.
