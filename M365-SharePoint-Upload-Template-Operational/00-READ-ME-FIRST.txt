HESEM QMS - SharePoint Publication Template
Governance revision: 2026-04-14

Purpose
SharePoint is a read-only publication and discovery surface for controlled
records that were accepted through the MOM/eQMS portal. SharePoint is not the
authoritative source of truth for final evidence, document revisions, form
submissions, or signatures.

Portal-first rules
- Users download form blanks, fill online forms, upload offline Excel carriers,
  request amendments, search evidence, review, approve, and sign through the
  portal only.
- End users must not upload controlled evidence directly to SharePoint.
- Offline Excel files are controlled capture carriers. The authoritative record
  is created only after portal issuance, upload validation, finalization, and
  canonical persistence.
- SharePoint publication is asynchronous after portal acceptance. A failed
  publication does not make the portal evidence invalid; it creates a
  publication state and retry/exception record.

Evidence package requirement
Every final evidence package must include:
1. Original artifact.
2. Canonical structured payload.
3. Readable snapshot.
4. Hash/signature manifest.
5. Publication receipt or publication state record.

Permissions model
- SharePoint site owners maintain read-only distribution libraries.
- Department users receive read access as needed for discovery.
- Edit/upload permissions are restricted to the controlled publication worker
  or approved system publishing identity.
- Direct department edit folders for final evidence are prohibited.

Operational note
If a user has a filled form or evidence file, route it to the portal upload or
online form flow. Do not place it directly into SharePoint and do not treat a
SharePoint file as the authoritative record.
