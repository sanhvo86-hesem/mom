# Translation Policy Batch 1 - 02-Tai-Lieu-He-Thong

- Pham vi: toan bo `02-Tai-Lieu-He-Thong`.
- Muc tieu: dich toan bo cac cum Anh-Viet dang chen trong van ban; khong dong vao `href`, ma tai lieu, file name, chuc danh cu the va ten phong ban.
- Nguyen tac chu thich lan dau:
  - Neu tuong duong tieng Viet da pho bien tren web/tai lieu Viet Nam, chi dung tieng Viet.
  - Neu tu Viet da dung duoc nhung do pho bien chua cao, lan dau trong moi tai lieu se giu dang `Tieng Viet (English)`.
- Nhom duoc xem la da pho bien, khong can giu English o lan dau:
  - `ra soat hop dong`
  - `bang kiem`
  - `huong dan cong viec`
  - `lenh san xuat`
  - `kiem soat truy cap`
  - `xem xet cua lanh dao`
- Nhom can giu English o lan dau de nguoi doc lien doi:
  - `thoi gian dan (lead time)`
  - `phe duyet giao hang (ship release)`
  - `bo ho so giao hang (ship packet)`
  - `xac nhan giao hang (ship confirm)`
  - `ho so cong viec (job dossier)`
  - `phieu theo doi cong viec (job traveler)`
  - `mau dau (first piece)`
  - `bo ho so ra soat (review pack)`
  - `bo ho so chuan (baseline package)`
  - `bo bang chung (evidence pack)`
  - `bang theo doi hanh dong (action tracker)`
  - `trung tam dieu hanh (control tower)`
- Dinh huong xu ly `owner`:
  - `process owner` -> `chu qua trinh`
  - `document owner` -> `chu tai lieu`
  - `data owner` -> `chu du lieu`
  - `business owner` -> `chu nghiep vu`
  - `owner` chung -> `nguoi phu trach`
- Dinh huong batch 1:
  - uu tien sua cac tai lieu he thong va handbook de giam "nguon sinh ngon ngu nua mua" truoc
  - sau khi chay batch 1 phai manual QA tren QMS manual va cac handbook co mat do term cao
