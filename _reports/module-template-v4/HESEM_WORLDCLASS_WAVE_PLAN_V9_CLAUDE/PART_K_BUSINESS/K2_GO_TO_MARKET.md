## 8. Customer success metrics (sales side)

```
Time to first value (TTFV)         < 30 days (Core);
                                  < 90 days (Pro / Enterprise)
Time to full deployment             4-8 mo (mid-market);
                                  12-18 mo (Enterprise);
                                  per pack overlay extension
                                  (per K1 §2 + I8 §2)
Pilot conversion rate                ≥ 60%
CSAT                                  ≥ 0.85
NPS                                    ≥ 50
NRR (Net Revenue Retention)            ≥ 110% (Pro);
                                  ≥ 115% (Enterprise)
GRR (Gross Revenue Retention)            ≥ 95% (logo retention)
Win rate                                  ≥ 30% on qualified opps
Average sales cycle                       per tier:
                                          Core 30-60 d;
                                          Pro 90-150 d;
                                          Enterprise 180-365 d
Pipeline coverage                          ≥ 4× quota
Per-pack expansion rate                    ≥ 30% within 12 mo
                                          of land
```
