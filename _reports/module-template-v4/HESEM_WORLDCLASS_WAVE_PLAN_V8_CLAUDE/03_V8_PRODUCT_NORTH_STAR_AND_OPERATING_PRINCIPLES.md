## 1. North Star (V7 carry-forward, V8 sharpened)

V7: "operations platform where every demand, change, move, action, inspection, nonconformance, CAPA, maintenance, release, AI recommendation has authority, evidence, lineage, workflow, policy, observability."

V8 sharpening: every operational fact about the manufacturing enterprise is **(a) generated by an authoritative root, (b) recorded in the OTG, (c) governed by a state machine, (d) substantiated by evidence, (e) gated by policy, (f) traceable to a workflow_event, (g) anchored to an immutable audit chain, (h) accountable to a named owner, (i) measurable against a published SLO**.

If any of (a)–(i) is missing for a fact in production, the platform is incomplete by definition.

### H1 — Pre-production readiness (months 3-12)

```yaml
goal:        18 Wave-1 roots reach L3-L4; eQMS core slices E2E green; OTG nodes/edges populated
exit_evidence:
  - 18 Wave-1 roots at L≥3
  - selected eQMS roots at L4 with live read-only graduated per ADR
  - OTG node count > 100K, edge count > 500K (synthetic + live)
  - audit chain anchor cron 100% PASS / 30d

  - per-route p95_latency_ms <= 500
  - error_rate_pct <= 0.5
  - axe_serious_violations == 0
quantitative_kpi:
  - L3 coverage of in-scope roots >= 0.30
  - L4 coverage of in-scope roots >= 0.10
```

### H2 — Controlled mutation (months 12-24)

```yaml
goal:        Stage 3 mutation graduation across 10+ surfaces; saga compensation tested
exit_evidence:
  - >= 10 transitions at L5 with per-mutation ADR
  - audit chain integrity 100% PASS (axiom A3, A6)
```
